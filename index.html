Studio Aashraya

Architecture for Sustainable Living

Studio Aashraya is an architecture practice dedicated to sustainable, contextual design in India. We build spaces that honor local heritage while eliminating the need for mechanical cooling.

Our Core Pillars

Net-Zero Design

Our buildings harness solar power on-site to meet 100% of their energy needs, leaving a footprint of deep respect for the environment.

Passive Strategies

Innovative natural ventilation, daylight optimization, and critical shading to eliminate the need for mechanical air-conditioning.

Vernacular Materials

We emphasize using local bamboo, earth, and brick to reduce our footprint and seamlessly harmonize the structure with its place.
